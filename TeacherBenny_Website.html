About Me

A teacher who truly listens

Hi, I'm Benny — an online English teacher with over 10 years of experience helping students of all ages find their voice in English. I believe learning a language should feel natural, enjoyable, and completely tailored to you.

Whether you're a young learner just starting out, an adult looking to build confidence in conversation, or someone who wants to strengthen their grammar and listening — I'm here to guide you every step of the way.

FAQ

Frequently asked questions

What age groups do you teach? +
I teach all ages! For children (ages 5–12) I offer fun, engaging kids ESL lessons. For teenagers and adults I cover grammar from A1 to B2, speaking, listening and writing. Every lesson is adapted to the student's age, level and goals.
Do I need to buy any books or materials? +
No! All lesson plans and learning materials are prepared and provided by me. You just show up and learn.
How do I pay? +
Payment is made in advance before your lessons begin. I'll send you full payment details once we've agreed on a schedule. I'll also provide a proper invoice or quote document for your records.
What if I need to cancel a lesson? +
No problem — just let me know at least 4 hours before the session. If you cancel on time, there's no charge and we'll reschedule to a time that suits everyone. Cancellations made with less than 4 hours notice will count as a lesson and be charged in full.
Which platform do we use for lessons? +
All lessons are held online via Microsoft Teams. You'll just need a device with a camera and microphone and a stable internet connection. I'll send you the meeting link before each session.
I'm a complete beginner. Can I still join? +
Of course! My General English lessons cover all levels from A1 to B2 — so whether you're just starting out or already have some English and want to build on it, you're very welcome. Lessons are never "just for beginners." We start exactly where you are.
